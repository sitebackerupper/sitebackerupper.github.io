Mist City: The Disappearance of Alice Liddell

Gauntlet Hangouts
Luiz June 7, 2019, 3:47am #1

Alice Liddell was, until a month ago, the rising star of the literary world.

And then she vanished.

The general public went wild with the mysterious disappearance. It was as if she just fell off the face of the earth. But you and your crew know better. You were there the night Alice stepped through the portal.

You saw her fall.

You saw her escape.

Mist City is a trimmed-down City of Mist hack that keeps the setting intact while focusing explicitly on investigation, action and life struggles through the use of light-weight mechanics.

Please note the first session is 4 hours long instead of 3, since we’ll be making characters
