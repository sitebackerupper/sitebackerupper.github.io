The is for four sessions of The Sword, the Crown, & the Unspeakable Power. Being able to attend each session is ideal, but not required. You must RSVP to each session you wish to attend. The sessions are scheduled for three hours, except for the first, which is four. The session will be streamed on Hangouts on Air and recorded for Gauntlet Hangouts.

The Sword, the Crown, & the Unspeakable Power focuses on power, intrigue, and shifting alliances in a dark fantasy setting. Inspired by political science-fiction and fantasy such as A Song of Ice and Fire by George R.R. Martin, The First Law by Joe Abercrombie, Imperial Radch by Ann Leckie, and the TV show Vikings, players explore the harsh realities of power, duty, servitude, and privilege as they command their subjects, fulfill the obligations of their station, or struggle against those above them, who would see them crushed beneath their boot.

We will use the X-card and Script Change during our sessions. We will observe the Gauntlet Inclusivity Policy.
