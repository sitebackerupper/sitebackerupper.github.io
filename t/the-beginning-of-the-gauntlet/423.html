Jeremy Strandberg, while archiving our G+ Community, found my first G+ message posted to The Gauntlet page. At that time, The Gauntlet was a tiny group for organizing DnD games in Houston. This message from me represents the very first step in creating The Gauntlet as we know it today. It’s a great find, and I’m really emotional about it.
