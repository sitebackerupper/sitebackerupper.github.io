Microscope/Kingdom: Kriusthos

Gauntlet Hangouts
Ludovico_Alves June 14, 2019, 12:43pm #1

This event is scheduled to run for 3 hours. It will be recorded assuming all parties agree.

Kriusthos is a fortified island city-state on the coast of the mythical continent of Elpis. It was founded three generations ago by the survivors of a supernatural apocalypse. It is assumed it is the last city in the world.

Here it is written how the City of the Ram came to be, as well as how it vanished back into history.

We come together to create the creation and fall of a city-state at the end of the world, in a setting inspired by early and middle Antiquity, as well as its internal dynamics. The city will be used for an Antiquity/mythological urban picturesque game.

Microscope (Explorer) and Kingdom will be used.

The Gauntlet Inclusivity Policy will be in play and safety tools (Script Change and X-Card) will be in use.
