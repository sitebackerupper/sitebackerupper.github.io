Hearts of Wulin

A tabletop roleplaying game of wuxia melodrama, Powered by the Apocalypse.

Hearts of Wulin is a game of wuxia melodrama, Powered by the Apocalypse. It’s by Agatha Cheng and Lowell Francis, featuring illustrations by Minerva Fox. It’s also the first standalone game to be published by The Gauntlet.

This project is extremely important to our community. The game was developed in our spaces and we’re hoping, if the Kickstarter is successful enough, it can serve as the springboard for a new publishing model, one that is less predatory than what the TTRPG industry has traditionally seen.

Please take a look at our campaign and consider backing it. We still have 2 stretch goals to unlock in the final hours. Thanks!
