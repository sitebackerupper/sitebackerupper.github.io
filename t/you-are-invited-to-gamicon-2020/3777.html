Hello!

Gamicon 2020 will run from Friday (Feb. 28) through Sunday (March 1) in Cedar Rapids, IA, at the Marriott (address below). Gamicon

The convention itself is a typical small to medium-sized Midwest gaming convention (400-500 attendees) with a fair amount of board and card games. D&D and Pathfinder are really big as well. Gamicon has been around for over 25 years.

The group that I’m part of, Corridor Games on Demand works to share indie and small press RPGs at local conventions and the downtown Cedar Rapids Public Library. We’ve been doing this since 2012 in the area.

https://tabletop.events/conventions/gamicon-copper

Cedar Rapids Marriott
1200 Collins Rd NE
Cedar Rapids, IA 52402
Phone: 319-393-6600

Please let me know what, if any additional questions you may have and I’ll try to answer them for you.
