These games are part of the ongoing Meadowsea series, a collection of tales inspired by the Redwall series of books starring brave little woodland critters set in an idyllic prairie landscape.

These one-shots are scheduled for 3 hours. These sessions will be recorded and shared. It is not necessary to attend each session, nor to have participated in previous sessions in the series. You must RSVP to each session you wish to attend. I will send a group message on Google Hangouts one week before the start of the session to touch base, and I will share a link to the call there shortly before start time.

We will use Script Change as a safety tool: http://www.briecs.com/p/script-change-rpg-tool.html
We will adhere to the Gauntlet’s Inclusivity Policy: http://www.gauntlet-rpg.com/the-inclusivity-policy.html
