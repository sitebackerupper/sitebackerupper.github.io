The societies of old Earth are gone. This is a new world, littered with the wreckage of what came before and run through with a strange new physics that feels almost like magic.

Songs for the Dusk is a Forged in the Dark hack about trying to rebuild in the ruins of a post-apocalyptic science-fantasy world.

You’ll play a crew of brave and compassionate adventurers who explore this strange new world and help people among the teeming threats that dot its landscape. Both magic and futuristic technology exist side by side. Want to be a robotic witch? You can! (And you should, because I can’t, being the GM and all.)
