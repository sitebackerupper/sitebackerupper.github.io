Pasión de las Pasiones is a PbtA tabletop role-playing game where you play the cast of the world’s most dramatic and exciting telenovela: Pasión de las Pasiones, as well as the family watching at home. In Pasión de las Pasiones, lies will be exposed, plots will be shattered, hearts will be broken, and if you’re lucky, you might even get a happy ending…at least for now. This is a game of melodrama, so be prepared for that.

This is for the first of three sessions using the ashcan version from Magpie Games. Sessions run about 2 1/2 hours. Attendance at all sessions is encouraged but not required. We will record the session for posting. If you sign up and your attendance or RSVP changes, please also send me a message at edige23 at gmail.

Note: I’m not an aficionado of telenovellas but I will do my due diligence beforehand. I hope my love of K-Dramas will carry over as well.
