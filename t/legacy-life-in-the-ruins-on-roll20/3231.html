How do you allow the players the ability to add things and modify the map? I know they can freehand, but I’ve always found it annoying that only the GM can drop art assets in.

technoskald September 26, 2019, 11:21am #3

Normally in Roll20 you are correct, only the GM can add tokens or assets to the canvas. You could set up a “palette” area to the side and add them there, then give players control of them and let them move things around. Alternatively they can use the drawing tools rather than tokens.
